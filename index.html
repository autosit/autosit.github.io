This page presents project- and master thesis proposals for the Autoferry project. The overall goal of the project is to build and demonstrate a passengerer ferry for autonomous urban water transport. The proposals are relevant for students from a wide range of departments at NTNU, inluding ITK, IMT, IE, ID and IIK.
